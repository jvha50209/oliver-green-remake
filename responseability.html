PACKAGING

Our packaging is made with a multiple purpose in mind. We wanted to create a sleek case that doubles as a transport and a presentation case. This means, that instead of the traditional inner box, outer box, plus transportation box, we have one box for all, lowering our materials use drastically. The box is made from premium FSC certified craft paper, with protective pyramid foam inside. To add further protection, we packed every component in recyclable aluminum bags.
